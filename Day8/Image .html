Ooty, Tamil Nadu

If the hill stations of the North are off your list now, then try taking a summer break in Ooty (also known as Udhagamandalam and short for Ootacamund) in Tamil Nadu. The mystical Blue Mountains of the Nilgiris, alluring tea gardens, serene waterfalls, and an expansive lake covering 65 acres are a paradise for a nature lover. The Toy Train of Ooty is famous for the panoramic views of this hill station in the south. The historic Nilgiri Mountain Railways is also a UNESCO world heritage site with as many as 16 tunnels and many bridges. Trek to the Doddabetta Peak for awesome scenic views from the top. Because it is an all-year-round destination, plan a visit to this picturesque hill station in South India whenever you feel like taking a break.
